Strategic Upgrade Paradigm
This matrix focuses on swapping out slow, color-locked, or low-utility assets for proactive cards that guarantee rapid ramp, immediate protection, or board-tilting card advantage engines. Every single recommendation adheres strictly to the No Infinite Combos policy.

Loyal Drake Consecrated Sphinx Mulldrifter Synergy & Engine High Drake is slow and dependent on Volo being out. Sphinx (Sphinx) or Mulldrifter (Elemental) offer explosive draw velocity that leverages Volo triggers instantly.

Junk Winder Koma, Cosmic Serpent Scourge of Fleets Win Conditions High Junk Winder requires heavy token setup. Koma (Serpent) locks down entire boards by itself and generates tokens on every single upkeep phase.
