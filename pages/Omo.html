Executive Summary

Land Ho is a highly synergistic, high-power casual engine deck that brilliantly exploits Omo, Queen of Vesuva's unique "Everything counter" mechanics. By placing counters that transform lands into all land types and creatures into all creature types, the deck builds a massive board state leveraging Locus clouds, Urza-tron pieces, and explosive typal scaling. The build operates with lethal strategic layer complexity but remains strictly within casual bounds by omitting infinite loop mechanics, opting instead for overwhelming combat overruns and massive X-spell draw engines.

Synergy & Engine

8.5 / 10

The core engine revolves around Omo, Queen of Vesuva distributing Everything counters to bridge unrelated powerful mechanics. Lands get access to every land type simultaneously, transforming standalone utility lands into hyper-charged structural backbones.

  • The Locus and Tron Payload: By giving Cloudpost or Glimmerpost an Everything counter, all other lands with counters suddenly count as additional Loci. This allows Cloudpost to tap for absurd amounts of mana. Similarly, Urza's Mine, Urza's Power Plant, and Urza's Tower instantly active their Urza-tron bonus text with just a single counter distributed between them or an active Planar Nexus.
  • Creature Amalgamation Loops: On the creature side, everything counters turn utility pieces into absolute powerhouses. Azami, Lady of Scrolls converts every creature with an everything counter into a Wizard capable of tapping to draw cards. Lord of Atlantis grants islandwalk and a stat buff to your entire board, while Herald of Hoofbeats renders your army unblockable via Horsemanship because they are technically Knights.
  • Value Amplifiers: Roaming Throne chosen as "Avatar" or "Shaman" doubles Omo's attack and enter-the-battlefield triggers, rapidly scaling counter distribution across the board. Displacer Kitten combined with low-cost spells blinks Omo or utility pieces to spread counters or reset ETB effects at instant speed.

Interaction & Board Control

7.0 / 10

The deck runs a robust interaction suite that leverages premier Simic countermagic and flexible target removal, though it lacks heavy repeatable board wipes, leaning heavily into proactive defense.

  • High-Tier Stack Control: Fierce Guardianship and Mana Drain provide high-tempo protection. Mana Drain pulling triple duty by protecting a key piece like Mana Reflection or Reshape the Earth and using the converted mana to hardcast Apex Devastator on the subsequent turn.
  • Targeted Removal Suite: Traditional, highly reliable interactive options such as Beast Within and Pongify deal with immediate threats cleanly. Curse of the Swine acts as an exile-based mass-removal spell that lets you systematically pick apart problematic boards while leaving your utility pieces unhindered.
  • Instant-Speed Board Protection: Heroic Intervention protects the explosive tokens generated by Scute Swarm or Avenger of Zendikar against standard toxic sweepers. Talon Gates of Madara acts as a brilliant utility land that can phase out an opposing commander or shield your own.

Consistency & Tutors

7.5 / 10

Consistency is driven by deep card-draw velocity and heavy land-specific tutoring. The list easily finds its specific silver-bullet lands to establish game-winning states.

  • Targeted Land Search: Expedition Map, Hour of Promise, and Scapeshift are the premier tutors here. They allow the deck to bypass traditional variance to instantly pull out Dark Depths, Thespian's Stage, or the devastating Field of the Dead.
  • Card Draw Velocity: The combination of Aesi, Tyrant of Gyre Strait and Tatyova, Benthic Druid alongside land-drop enablers guarantees that your hand remains completely full. Mass draw from Finale of Revelation and Drown in Dreams transforms raw mana into total card dominance.
  • Sylvan Library & Filtering: Early game consistency is shored up by Sylvan Library, allowing you to dig deep into your deck to find required ramp tools or protective elements before Omo comes down.

Speed & Mana Base

8.0 / 10

The speed of this deck is highly volatile but explosive, scaling exponentially based on how quickly its custom mana engines are assembled.

  • Explosive Velocity: With Dryad of the Ilysian Grove or Azusa effects, combined with Scapeshift, the deck can jump from 5 available mana to 20+ in a single turn cycle. Reshape the Earth acts as an absolute mid-game reset button that pulls out 10 customized utility lands to instantly win or lock down the board.
  • Mana Base Composition: The land base is highly specialized, trading traditional basic land speed for extreme late-game utility. Simic Growth Chamber allows you to pick up and replay high-value utility lands like Boseiju, Who Endures or re-trigger landfall targets.
  • Engine Scaling: Mana Reflection combined with Cloudpost and multiple everything counters results in individual lands tapping for 10+ mana each, enabling massive hardcast threats well ahead of standard curves.

Win Conditions & Closeness to "Infinite"

6.5 / 10

The deck respects the No Infinite Combos restriction completely. It functions as a fair but terrifyingly powerful combat-focused and token-driven closing engine.

  • Token Swarm Strategy: Traditional win-conditions include making hundreds of insect tokens via Scute Swarm or plants via Avenger of Zendikar, then swinging for lethal overruns. Awaken the Woods converts raw mana directly into an army of Land Creatures that trigger all landfall text simultaneously.
  • Marit Lage & Copy Outlets: Replicating Dark Depths via Thespian's Stage or Mirage Mirror produces a 20/20 flying indestructible avatar instantly. Adding an everything counter to Dark Depths also allows you to perform unique copy synergies to bypass counter removals.
  • Unblockable Typal Slams: Utilizing Herald of Hoofbeats to grant Horsemanship or Lord of Atlantis for Islandwalk turns an otherwise blockable token army into an immediate, lethal combat step against the entire table.
