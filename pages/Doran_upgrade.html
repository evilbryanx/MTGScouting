Upgrade Optimization Strategy

This Tactical Upgrade Matrix is the result of a rigorous multi-pass filtration process aimed at maximizing operational velocity, reducing high-mana clunkiness, and enhancing engine fluidity. We have targeted the 10 lowest-velocity assets in the current decklist and provided optimized Unrestricted and Budget alternatives. The core objective is to decrease the average mana curve while adding immediate interaction, better card velocity, and tighter combat finishers.
