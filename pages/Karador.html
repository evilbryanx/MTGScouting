Executive Summary

Grave News is a highly optimized Abzan Reanimator-Control engine that leverages extreme self-mill and recursive sacrifice loops to continuously generate card advantage and suppress opponents. It functions as a complete graveyard toolbox, using the commander to bypass traditional resource constraints. It finishes tables via explosive non-combat damage burst loops or protected creature-based infinite combos.

1. Synergy & Engine 8.5 / 10

The deck runs a world-class recursive backbone that completely exploits the graveyard as an extension of the hand.

  • Self-Mill Operations: Absolute velocity is achieved through premium self-mill enablers including Stitcher's Supplier, Aftermath Analyst, Nyx Weaver, and the massive dredge value of Golgari Grave-Troll.
  • Draw & Selection Filters: Underrealm Lich forms a massive card selection core that avoids formal draw steps, dodging drawing penalties completely. Greater Good, Altar of Dementia, Viscera Seer, and Skullclamp turn bodies into high card velocity.
  • Massive Draw Loops: Flinging or sacrificing high-power bodies like Lord of Extinction or Doom Weaver directly to Greater Good allows you to instantly map and move your entire deck into accessible zones.
Tactical Hazard Warning: The entire operational velocity depends heavily on the graveyard. Unanswered external exile static pieces like Rest in Peace or Leyline of the Void will instantly collapse your strategic engines.
2. Interaction & Board Control 7.5 / 10

Control is deployed symmetrically and asymmetrically via highly repeatable, creature-attached spell effects.

  • Targeted Operations: Flexible single-target removal is heavily distributed through recursive creature bodies like Haywire Mite, Archon of Justice, and Ravenous Chupacabra, with Tear Asunder offering crucial non-creature exile safety features.
  • Edict Dominance: Accursed Marauder provides low-cost, multi-player sacrifice removal that clean-sweeps hexproof, shroud, and indestructible combat threats easily.
  • Asymmetric Board Resets: The list pairs creature wipe threats like False Prophet directly with free sacrifice outlets, establishing an instant-speed, repeatable exile wipe loop that leaves your graveyard fully intact to rebuild instantly.
3. Consistency & Tutors 8.0 / 10

The structural layout is highly consistent, utilizing premier black and green tutoring paths to assemble engines smoothly.

  • Unconditional Tutors: Demonic Tutor provides seamless, unconditional top-tier searching capability to fix any operational gap.
  • Survival Engine: Survival of the Fittest represents the ultimate engine piece in this list, turning dead creature cards or active dredgers like Stinkweed Imp directly into silver-bullet answers or instant winning combinations.
  • Direct-To-Field Packages: Gravebreaker Lamia and Sidisi, Undead Vizier accurately place options across zones, while a resolving Protean Hulk acts as an instant game-ending threat by pulling precise toolboxes or infinite assemblies straight to the board.
4. Speed & Mana Base 7.0 / 10

The land layout features elite optimization using premium mana-fixing tools and highly synergized creature ramp pipelines.

  • Land Base Optimization: Smooth fixing is guaranteed via original duals (Bayou, Savannah, Scrubland), shock lands (Overgrown Tomb, etc.), fetch lands, and Horizon pathways.
  • Creature-Based Ramp: The mana acceleration relies safely on recursive bodies like Sakura-Tribe Elder and Wood Elves, paired perfectly with land-from-graveyard systems driven by Aftermath Analyst and Life from the Loam.
  • High Velocity Utility Lands: Phyrexian Tower and Diamond Valley provide incredible high-utility sacrifice options, yielding huge bursts of mana or sudden life gains.
5. Win Conditions & Closeness to "Infinite" 7.0 / 10

The list shifts efficiently between high-power creature damage lines and definitive non-combat loop sequences.

  • Explosive Fling Outlets: Jarad, Golgari Lich Lord serves as an instant win condition, flinging massive threats like Lord of Extinction or Golgari Grave-Troll to burst down the entire table simultaneously.
  • Aristocrat Drain Loops: Rapid death triggers from pieces like Kokusho, the Evening Star or Junji, the Midnight Sky quickly drain opposing life totals down to zero.
  • Infinite Assembly Lines: The inclusion of the classic Karmic Guide + Reveillark line provides an infinite execution loop when paired with free sacrifice outlets, allowing you to infinitely recur power 2-or-less targets like Stitcher's Supplier to completely empty your library, or Accursed Marauder to wipe clean all opposing fields.
